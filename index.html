Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2026 · Version: 1.0.0 · com.M77med.flexigrid

Information We Collect

None

FlexiGrid does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, device information, or usage data. The app operates entirely offline and all grid calculations are performed locally on your device. No account creation, registration, or login is required. We do not use cookies, trackers, analytics, or any form of data collection mechanism.

How We Use Information

Since FlexiGrid collects no information, there is no data to use, process, or analyze. The app functions solely based on user input provided directly through the interface, which remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

None

FlexiGrid does not share any data with third parties because no data is collected. We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user information. The app does not integrate with any advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media services.

Data Retention

As FlexiGrid does not collect any personal data, there is no data to retain. All user settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using shared preferences and can be cleared at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling the application.

Children's Privacy

COPPA Compliant

FlexiGrid is safe for users of all ages. The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. No data is collected, stored, or shared, making the app fully compliant with children's privacy regulations including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored remotely, there are minimal security risks. All app preferences are stored locally on your device using the standard shared preferences mechanism provided by the operating system. We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated for general security best practices.

Third-Party Services

None

FlexiGrid does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app functions completely offline and requires no internet connection for core functionality. No data is sent to any external server or service.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app and the effective date will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage users to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through the official Google Play Store listing page for FlexiGrid. We are committed to addressing your inquiries promptly.
